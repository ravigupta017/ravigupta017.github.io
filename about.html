Our Founders

Both founders came together to form this company Brand Radiator recognising that the small cities are home to entrepreneurs too. While coming to building start-up ecosystem, people always move towards metros and cosmopolitan cities, They miss the fact that actual India breaths in B & C category states/cities and to empower India digitally it is important to work in such locations where there is dearth of technologies. The founders share that there is lack of good digital marketing company in tier 2 and tier 3 states/towns with very little understanding on importance of the sector. Therefore, the desired benefits and impact on business or people is not visible. The founders of Brand Radiator, have taken a step forward, knowing the challenges, to revolutionise digital marketing in Bihar and every such state and making people aware of digital marketing as well as support realising the dream of Digital India also help in generating employment so that talents of the state do not go out in search of job.
