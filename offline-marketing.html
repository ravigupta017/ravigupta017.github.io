Offline Marketing

We might be the best digital marketing agency, but our offline marketing skills are equally strong and evident. Our traditional marketing campaigns never fail to leave an impact on people. Since traditional marketing can never go out of fashion, we operate through a system which works appropriately for your brand activation.

Services we provide

Events: Public events create mass awareness. We can develop as many engagements we want for the activation of your brand. Events act as a successful medium for potential audience or consumers as they get the live opportunity to try and test your brand in a personal interaction. Be it an exhibition stall design or a full-scale brand activation project, Brand Radiator is always on its toes to take it up for you.

Print: Avid readers contribute a lot to the print media. Newspapers, magazines and journals share potential space that can help your brand get a bigger picture on the horizon of marketing. If your advertisement is unable to find a striking space in the newspaper or magazine, then it’s bound to reap no results at all. Thus, we help your brand in image building by channelising responsive communication and creative root.

Brand Radiator’s creative team is always ready to take up challenges. Be it Newspapers, Magazines, Journals or any other print medium, we are experts in creating buzz through are influencing campaigns.

Video Production: In an offline marketing gimmick, visual storytelling concept can never let you down. Humans get connected emotionally to the messages experienced visually. Videos can encapsulate all of the above and even more if it’s done rightly. If you’re looking to convey your brand story or a product or a business concept through a visual story or animation, then, our production team would love to add convincing visual to your brand.

Outdoor: Outdoor advertisements are a creative & expensive venture which offers you an incredible medium to lock your products in the minds of the masses. Outdoor advertisements have pros and cons also, either it leaves an impact or drains your budget. Making sure that your outdoor budget harvests utility for your brand, Brand Radiator puts all the efforts to conceptualise designs for outdoor advertisements.

Media Planning: team makes sure that your business gets the best ad space at better rates in your most favoured publication. Expenditure on media, publication and communication demands appropriate budgeting and meticulous & creative planning.

A good sense of consumer behaviour, publications and ROIs enables us prepare and execute your media plans and ensure a strong ROI.

Brief our team about your brand and we will assure you a complete set up of campaigns and media plans for an impeccable activation of your brand.
